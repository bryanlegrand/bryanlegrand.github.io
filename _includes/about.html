About Me

How did I get into the world of Data?

  • 2004-2008

    Thinking Big

    Excited about IT since I was young, I first went to university at RIT for software development. After one year, I realized, as my English professor wrote, "Bryan wants to engage with bigger questions". I decided that software development didn't engage with bigger questions and ultimately transferred to Elon University where I double majored in Philosophy and Political Science.

  • 2008-2013

    Traveler, ESL Teacher

    Upon finishing my degrees I wanted to travel and see the world. I worked primarily as an English as a Second Language Teacher; where I spent time living in Morocco, South Korea, the Czech Republic, France, and finally landing on Reunion Island in 2013 where I continue to live today.
