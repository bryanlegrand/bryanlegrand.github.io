About Me

How did I get to the world of Data?

  • 2004-2008

    Thinking Big

    Excited about IT since I was young, I first went to university at RIT for software development. After one year, I realized that as my English professor wrote "Bryan wants to engage with bigger questions". I decided that programming lacked this big level engagement and ultimately transferred to Elon University where I double majored in Philosophy and Political Science.

  • 2008-2013

    Traveler, ESL Teacher

    Upon finishing my degrees I wanted to travel and see the world. I worked primarily as an English as a Second Language Teacher; where I spent time living in Morocco, South Korea, the Czech Republic, France, and finally landing in Reunion Island where I live to this day.

  • 2013-2022

    Freelancer, Entrepreneur, Developper

    Having permanently moved to Reunion Island in 2013, I worked for 5 years as a Freelance English Teacher before opening my own language school in 2018. American Way Formation grew from a part time side-gig to a team of 6 teachers and more than 100 individual students, a success story built largely on the back of a self-developed ERP and automatization solutions.

  • 2021-????

    Curiousity & Data, the perfect marriage

    A chance opportunity in mid 2021 introduced me to the world of Data by working as a freelance Data Analyst for a new consulting firm. I quickly realized that Data Analysis represented the perfect blend of my skills and passions. A curiousity to ask why, find and demand evidence, and a passion for new and evolving technology. Looking for new challenges and shift away from Teaching, I'm confident that I've found a new home in the world of Data
